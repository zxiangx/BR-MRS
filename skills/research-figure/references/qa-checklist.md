# QA Checklist

- Narrative: Do the trends and rankings match the stated claim?
- Realism: Are curves non-identical with mild local variation and plausible gaps?
- Readability: Labels, units, and ticks are clear and not crowded.
- Aesthetics: Colors are distinct and balanced; legend does not overlap data.
- Output: Save PNG (dpi=300) and PDF; output path and filenames match the request.
